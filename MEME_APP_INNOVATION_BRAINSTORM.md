## 💡 BRAINSTORMING FRAMEWORK

### How to Evaluate Ideas:

**Score each idea (1-5):**
- **Viral Potential:** Will users share it?
- **Retention Impact:** Will it bring users back?
- **Monetization:** Can you make money from it?
- **Development Effort:** How hard to build?
- **Uniqueness:** Does it differentiate from competitors?

**Priority = (Viral + Retention + Uniqueness) / Effort**
