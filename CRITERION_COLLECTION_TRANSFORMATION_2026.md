## 🎨 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### **Visual Language:**
- **Generous white space** - Let content breathe
- **Serif typography** - Literary, refined
- **Muted palette** - Sophisticated, not loud
- **Subtle shadows** - Depth without drama
- **Smooth transitions** - Intentional, not jarring

### **Interaction Philosophy:**
- **Weighted** - Animations feel like they have mass
- **Purposeful** - Every interaction means something
- **Quick** - Responsive, not slow
- **Smooth** - Continuous, not jumpy

### **Copy Tone:**
- **Literary** - Well-written, evocative
- **Thoughtful** - Shows intelligence
- **Unpretentious** - Smart without being snobby
- **Playful** - Serious about memes, not serious in tone

## 📝 NOTES & LEARNINGS

### Design Decisions:
- **Collections over categories** - More evocative, aspirational
- **Signals over stats** - Context over numbers
- **Taste over DNA** - Literary description vs gamification
- **Optional context** - Don't force education

### User Psychology:
- People want to feel cultured
- Scarcity creates value
- Context adds meaning
- Quality > Quantity resonates

### Technical Approach:
- Build on existing diversity engine
- Layer curation on top of algorithm
- Progressive enhancement
- A/B test everything
